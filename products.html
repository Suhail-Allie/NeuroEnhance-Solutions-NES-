Industry Standard Concentrations

These are some industry standard ranges for omega-3 fatty acids in common oil sources, though specific quantities may vary:

  • Cannabis Seed Oil: Between 50 and 60 percent alpha-linolenic acid (ALA)
  • Seaweed Oil: might differ greatly depending on the species, but a typical range is between 10 and 20 percent eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA) and 20 and 30 percent docosahexaenoic acid (DHA).

Note: These are rough estimates, and actual concentrations may vary depending on the specific source and extraction methods.

Calculating Oil Requirements

We can calculate the necessary dosages of each oil based on these industry requirements as well as our goal ratio of 1000 mg ALA, 250 mg EPA, and 250 mg DHA for consumers seeking overall health and wellness.

  • Cannabis Seed Oil: Assuming a 50% concentration, we would require about 2 grams of cannabis seed oil to produce 1000 mg of ALA.
  • Seaweed Oil: Assuming a 15% concentration for EPA and a 25% concentration for DHA, we would require roughly 8.33 grams of seaweed oil to obtain 250 mg of EPA and 250 mg of DHA.

Note: These are rough estimates based on industry averages. The actual amount of oil needed will depend on the precise ratios of the components you select.

Product Formulations: Powder, Liquid, and Capsule

Understanding Product Forms

Prior to going into particular computations, let's think about the effects of each product form:

Powder Form:

  • Advantages: Simple to combine with food or drinks, offering a range of flavors.
  • Challenges: Needs precise dosage; texture can be less appealing.

Liquid Form:

  • Advantages: Simple to eat, with the option for many flavors.
  • Challenges:Stability problems, possibility for oxidation, requires adequate packing.

Capsule Form:

  • Advantages: Easy, accurate dosage that prevents chemicals from oxidizing.
  • Challenges:Cost of producing the capsules may make it less tempting to customers who prefer other formats.

Assumptions:

  • We are going to utilize a powder foundation that has minimal effect on the omega-3 level.
  • The recommended daily allowance of 1000 mg ALA, 250 mg EPA, and 250 mg DHA is still in place.

Calculations:

  • We calculated about 2 grams of cannabis seed oil and 8.33 grams of seaweed oil for the capsule form based on earlier measurements.

Challenges:

  • Without the right measuring equipment, it might be challenging to ensure exact dosage.
